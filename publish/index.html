KisledScan

KisledScan is a lightweight and efficient barcode scanning app built with Expo. It goes beyond scanning by enabling webhook integration (n8n, Make.com, and custom backends), manual data entry, photo capture, and image upload.

Privacy Policy

KisledScan does not collect or store any personal data. All data captured via barcode, camera, or manual input is processed locally or sent only to user-defined webhooks. No data is shared with third parties.
